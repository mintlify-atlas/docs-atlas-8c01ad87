Satochip is a JavaCard-based hardware wallet that stores your Bitcoin and cryptocurrency private keys securely inside a physical smart card. Satochip-Utils is the desktop companion application that lets you initialize, seed, and fully manage your Satochip card from a single interface — no command line required.

## What you can do with Satochip-Utils

Satochip-Utils covers every stage of your Satochip card's lifecycle:

- **PIN setup** — Protect your card with a 4–16 character PIN during first-time initialization.
- **Seed import or generation** — Load an existing 12 or 24-word BIP39 mnemonic, or generate a new one directly on your computer, with optional BIP39 passphrase support.
- **Label** — Give your card a human-readable name to distinguish it from other cards.
- **Change PIN** — Update your PIN at any time once the card is set up.
- **Edit label** — Rename your card whenever you need to.
- **Check authenticity** — Verify that your card is genuine by inspecting its Root CA, Sub CA, and Device certificates.
- **Card information** — View the applet version, NFC status, seeding status, 2FA status, and remaining PIN tries.
- **Factory reset** — Wipe the card back to its factory state, erasing all keys, settings, and data.
