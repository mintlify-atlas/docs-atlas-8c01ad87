Satochip-Utils is your one-stop tool for managing the full Satochip card ecosystem. Whether you are setting up a Satochip hardware wallet, storing secrets on a Seedkeeper, or managing a Satodime bearer card, this application brings every feature together into a single desktop interface available on Windows, macOS, and Linux.

## What you can do with Satochip-Utils

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Satochip" icon="wallet">
    Set a PIN, label your card, import or generate a BIP39 seed phrase, verify authenticity, and factory reset.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Seedkeeper" icon="vault">
    Store and retrieve passwords, mnemonics, 2FA secrets, wallet descriptors, and raw data. Back up secrets to a second card.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Satodime" icon="tag">
    View card information, edit labels, and verify the authenticity of your Satodime bearer card.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the application">
    Download the latest release for your operating system from [GitHub](https://github.com/Toporin/Satochip-Utils/releases) and follow the [installation guide](/getting-started/installation).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your card reader">
    Plug in a compatible USB smart card reader and insert your Satochip, Seedkeeper, or Satodime card. See [hardware requirements](/getting-started/hardware-requirements) for supported devices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Satochip-Utils">
    Open the application. It automatically detects your card and shows the appropriate menu for your card type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up or manage your card">
    Follow the on-screen wizard to set a PIN, import a seed, or manage your secrets. Refer to the relevant section of this documentation for detailed guidance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The Satochip card ecosystem includes three distinct hardware products. Each card serves a different purpose — read the overview pages to understand which card fits your needs before purchasing.
</Note>
